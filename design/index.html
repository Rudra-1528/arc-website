About ARC

Welcome to the Autonomous Robotics Center (ARC). We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of robotics through innovation and collaboration. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge solutions that address real-world challenges, creating a smarter and more automated future.

We work closely with VIPs and clients to showcase the products we develop and our research topics. Our work with national defense and various faculty members highlights our commitment to impactful research and development. This website serves as a portfolio of our efforts, from new problem statements to the impressive work of our students.

Our Research Areas

Our research spans a variety of critical fields, addressing complex problems with innovative robotic solutions.

Autonomous Navigation

Developing intelligent systems that can navigate complex and dynamic environments without human intervention.

Human-Robot Interaction

Creating intuitive and safe interaction methods for seamless collaboration between people and robots.

AI & Machine Learning

Leveraging artificial intelligence to enable robots to learn, adapt, and make decisions in real-time.

Robotics in Medicine

Innovating robotic solutions for surgical assistance, diagnostics, and patient care.

Projects & Portfolio

Our portfolio showcases the practical application of our research through various projects and products.

Automated Manufacturing Arm

A high-precision robotic arm designed to enhance efficiency and safety in industrial settings.

Search & Rescue Drones

Urban Mobility Bot
